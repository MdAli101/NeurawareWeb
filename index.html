Clinical Accuracy.

Neuraware Systems builds AI-powered medical devices on FPGA-based platforms that detect cardiac abnormalities with clinical-grade accuracy—bringing hospital-level heart diagnostics to the edge. Backed by the Government of India's Chip to Startup (C2S) Programme.

Why Neuraware

Silicon meets stethoscope.

Heart disease is the world's leading cause of death. Early detection saves lives—but access to accurate diagnostics remains unequal. We're changing that. Our FPGA-based AI platform brings real-time cardiac analysis to the point of care, no cloud required. Just the chip, the patient, and the answer.

  • Edge AI Inference

    01 / 04

    Our models run directly on hardware. No internet. No latency. No compromises. AI inference at the edge means real-time ECG analysis—anywhere, anytime.

  • FPGA-Based SoC Platform

    02 / 04

    Purpose-built silicon. Our FPGA-based System-on-Chip is designed from the ground up for medical-grade signal processing and AI acceleration.

  • Clinical-Grade Accuracy

    03 / 04

    We don't approximate. Our AI models are trained and validated to deliver diagnostic accuracy that meets clinical standards—bringing hospital-grade results to a portable device.

  • Efficient by Design

    04 / 04

    Supported by the Government of India's Chip to Startup programme. Built in India. Designed for the world. Advancing self-reliance in semiconductor and MedTech.

Our focus

One mission. Every heartbeat counts.

We sit at the intersection of three revolutions: artificial intelligence, semiconductor design, and precision medicine. Each one is transforming its field. Together, they can save millions of lives.
